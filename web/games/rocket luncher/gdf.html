Life and Learning3

If you're young and exploring new things like using a PC, preparing for a future job, or like me, learning coding, you know how hard and stressful it can be.

And because it's easier said than done, you might find yourself just asking your parents to "buy this, that."

But if you spend all your time relaxing, playing video games, and doing nothing productive, it can have consequences.

When you reach the age where you need a job, you might realize you lack the necessary experience. You can play games, but you don't know how to create files, use other editor apps, or develop important skills.
